From the farmers to our consumers, our target is to make things easy for everyone! Our service brings comfort
to the customers as they can order vegetables/cereals and other farm product on whatsapp itself. It definitely saves their time and effort and this also ensures them fresh products.
Now coming to the another picture of our service (honestly the most important one) is the growth of farmers! Main reason behind to build this project was to eliminate the role of middle man and add marketing as a part of farming so that farmers can get the real value for their hardwork.
This will enhance the social and financial position of our farmers in our society. They can directly interact with their consumers, understand market, socialize and increase participation. Our project is not only what I have mentioned above.
